The Advanced First Aid Response System

Welcome to AFAR, a crowdsourced, geolocation-based, flexible software solution designed to save lives in medical emergencies.



How does it work?

  1. Find a restaurant that is a partner of Lineless
  2. Buy the food you want and pay for it
  3. When the order is almost done, you will receive a text message to notify you
  4. Pick up your food and enjoy it to go or to stay
